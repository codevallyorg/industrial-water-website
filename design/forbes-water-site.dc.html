About Forbes Water

Independent water specialists, accountable only to your outcomes

For more than 18 years, Forbes Water has helped Australian industry, agriculture and government get more from every drop — combining scientific rigour, field-proven engineering and genuinely independent advice.

Built in the field, not the boardroom

Forbes Water began with a simple observation: too many organisations were sold water treatment they didn't need, while the problems that actually cost them money went undiagnosed. We set out to be different — specialists who start with the water itself, not a product catalogue.

Today our team supports more than 340 sites nationwide, from remote mine dewatering operations to municipal drinking water schemes. Because we're independent, every recommendation is judged on one measure only: whether it delivers for you.

Our people are chemists, process engineers and environmental scientists who spend as much time on site as they do at a desk — which is exactly how we like it.

# Forbes Water — SEO & Information Architecture Map
Design system: **1a Deep Current** · Prototype: `forbes-water-site.dc.html`
All 20 pages are live in the prototype via the URLs (slugs) below.

```
Home
├── About
├── Services ─ 8 detail pages
├── Industries ─ 5 detail pages
├── Sustainability

```

## Page-by-page SEO

### Home — `/`
- **Title:** Forbes Water | Independent Water Treatment & Consulting Australia
- **H1:** Innovative water solutions for industry, communities & the environment
- **Meta:** Independent water consulting, treatment, monitoring and management across Australia. Optimise water quality, cut costs and stay compliant. Request a consultation.
- **Internal links:** all services, all industries, projects, contact.

### About — `/about`
- **Title:** About Us | Forbes Water
- **H1:** Independent water specialists, accountable only to your outcomes
- **Meta:** Meet Forbes Water — independent Australian water specialists with 18+ years' experience across 340+ sites. Chemists, engineers and scientists who start with the water, not a product catalogue.

- **H1:** Water consulting, treatment & management services
